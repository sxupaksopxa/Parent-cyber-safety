## Product Description

Parent Cyber Safety is a privacy-first digital safety assessment tool designed to help parents better understand and manage their children’s online environment.

The application guides parents through a short, structured questionnaire covering devices, apps, and online behavior. Based on the input, it generates a personalized safety report with clear, practical recommendations tailored to real-life situations.

Unlike traditional technical checklists, the tool combines:
- **Device configuration guidance** (e.g., privacy settings, permissions)
- **Behavioral recommendations** (e.g., communication, content sharing)
- **Conversation support** (age-appropriate guidance for parents and children)

The goal is to make digital safety approachable, actionable, and non-judgmental — helping families build awareness and safer habits together.

The application runs entirely in the browser and does not collect or store personal data, ensuring a fully privacy-first experience.
